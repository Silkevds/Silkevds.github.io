HOME                    PORTRAITS                    MEAT                    ARTIST                    GHOSTS

My so called 'ghosts' series is a series of paintings mainly created for experimentational purposes. I felt creatively exhausted for a while and so I decided to try something completely new, which resulted into this. The figures stand for feeling suffocated, unable to breathe, closed off from the world unable to see. These are pulled completely out of their context by the strangely upbeat, cheerful colours, or busy background. Creating a shift that distracts you from the actual heaviness of what's actually portrayed here.
